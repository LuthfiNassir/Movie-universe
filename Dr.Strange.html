Dr.Strange: Multiverse Of Madness

Overview:

Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness is a 2022 superhero film, based on the Marvel Comics superhero of the same name. The film is a sequel to Doctor Strange, Avengers: Endgame, WandaVision and Spider-Man: No Way Home. It is the twenty-eighth film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe and the eleventh installment of Phase Four. Doctor Strange teams up with a mysterious teenage girl from his dreams who can travel across multiverses, to battle multiple threats, including other-universe versions of himself, which threaten to wipe out millions across the multiverse.
